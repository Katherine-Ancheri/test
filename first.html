BIRTH AND NATIVITY

On JULY 7, 2000 I was born at St.George Hospital,Muvattupuzha,Ernakulam district.

My parents are Mr.Boban Mathew and Mrs.Mini Boban. I have an elder sister whose name is Irene B Ancheri.And we are settled at Adimaly,Idukki district which is also my native place. I have lived in Kerala only for three years maximum but it is the palce where I connect the most.Afterall home is where the heart is ..and my heart lies in my paradise.

Upto the age of 4 we were residing in Gwalior,Madhya Pradesh,Owing to my dad's service in THE INDIAN AIRFORCE.After that we were there in kerala for three years before moving to Hyderabad for another three years.

By 2012 we came to TamilNadu,Tirunelveli district. And there I spend seven most terrific and terrible years of my life.It wasn't all beds of roses;more like throne of thorns.
