KATHERINE B ANCHERI

It has been two decades since I came upon this realm.What good thing I did over these years, on coming to think of it, other than surviving so far I can't think of anything else. Dreams were big, ambition bigger and laziness the biggest..

This work will remain as a recollection of everything I went through in the years gone by.Actually maybe not everything; because I believe that we must always not tell everyone what happens in our life, if we do so then there wont be a spark of life.The less told, the more exciting life is.....
